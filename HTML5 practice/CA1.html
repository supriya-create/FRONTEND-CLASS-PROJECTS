Delicious Pasta Recipe

Follow this simple recipe to make a delicious homemade pasta dish.



Instructions

  1. Boil water and add salt.
  2. Add pasta and cook until al dente.
  3. Prepare the sauce with tomatoes and herbs.
  4. Mix pasta with sauce and serve hot.

Ingredients

✔️ 200g Pasta

✔️ 2 Tomatoes

✔️ 1 tsp Olive Oil

✔️ Salt & Pepper
